## Ingredients

- 2 boiled chicken breasts
- 1 cup flour
- 2/3 cup milk
- 3 tsp baking powder
- ¼ tsp salt

## Directions

- Break chicken into 2” pieces.
- In a bowl, beat with whisk the remaining ingredients. Heat ½” oil in frying pan (or use deep fryer).
- Dip chicken in batter & sauté until golden brown.
